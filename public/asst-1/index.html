What makes a good water bottle?

Lets find out!

  1. Size

    The size of a water bottle matters! It has to be:

    • Small enough to travel with
    • Large enough to hold a good amount of water
    • The perfect size for you!

  2. Volume

    The volume of a water bottle matters! It has to be:

    • Larger than 12oz: no more baby bottles here
    • Smaller than 64oz: leave those for Shrek
    • The perfect volume for you!

  3. Color

    The color of a water bottle matters! It has to be:

    • Not too dark: it could blend in too much and you could lose it
    • Not too bright: it could draw a lot of attention and someone could steal it!
    • The perfect color for you!
